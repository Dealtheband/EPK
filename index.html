About

DEAL is a Norwegian Americana trio based in southeastern Norway. Their sound is built around electric and acoustic instruments, vocal harmonies, and honest storytelling.

Drawing on the deep roots of American music — country, blues and folk — DEAL has developed a sound that is direct, warm, and distinctly their own.

The band has played everything from intimate club nights to major festivals, sharing stages with artists such as Malin Pettersen, Erlend Gunstveit, Byting and American songwriter Grayson Capps.

"Direct and honest — built around vocal harmonies and storytelling."
